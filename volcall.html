TEDx AJCE ​Volunteer Call

The TEDx AJCE ​ Club is expected to admit 24 students of AJCE to its volunteering panel. The various categories for the application of students are as follows

Production team

The team will manage all production and planning for the day of the event. They oversee all of the roles happening on the day of your event related to content production, stage management, technology, and video.

Event Management Team

The team manages most of the event’s day-of logistics, and is responsible for creating a “TEDx experience” for our guests. They serve as the contact with the venue’s staff, manage day-of event activities outside of the main stage, and are in charge of ticketing and registration needs.

Design Team

TEDx events are always bright and creative, the design team members are expected to further induce innovative and diverse ideas into the stage setup, banners, ID cards, Certificates, placards, Posters, souvenirs and websites. All the designing, acquirement of materials and arrangement are expected to be done by the design team. Previous works or a link to your portfolio would be preferred to be mailed to ​tedxajce@gmail.com​

Editorial Team

TEDx events are regularly updated on their pages on social media and the official TEDx Website along with a final event report. The editorial team members are expected to fill up the reader’s heart with a good TEDx spirit. Previous works or a link to your portfolio would be preferred to be mailed to ​tedxajce@gmail.com​

Communication and Marketing Team

It’s important to have volunteers in a team to create and manage a strong online presence of your event, including website content, a blog and social media. The team is responsible for promoting your event to the public.

Video & Production Team

Introductory videos are played during the event and the entire session is recorded. The media team is expected to edit and publish videos according to the TEDx rules and regulations through the TEDx portal. Skills in video/photo editing, management and execution is required in this field. Previous works or a link to your portfolio would be preferred to be mailed to ​tedxajce@gmail.com​

The TEDx event is expected to be held during April 2018.

Students applying for this club must keep the following guidelines in mind
  • There will be no registration fee for admission to this club
  • The event preparations will be fully operational during weekends and volunteers with respective duties will be required to stay back to meet the event deadline.
  • Pupil must have satisfactory communication skills in English and be enthusiastic enough to complete a given task .
  • This event will be the perfect exposure to both introverts and extroverts who wish to explore diversity apart from just engineering.
Conditions for application
  • You have to be a student currently pursuing a UG/PG course at Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, Kanjirapally.
  • You can apply for more than one position but only a single post will be allotted based on your skillset.
  • Your application has to be submitted on or before 26th January 2018
Disclaimer: The final decisions regarding selection of volunteers will be taken by the TEDx​AJCE Executive Team.
