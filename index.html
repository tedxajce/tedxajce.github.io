“Creative thinking inspires ideas. Ideas inspire change” - Barbara Januszkiewicz. TED is a nonprofit media organization devoted to spreading ideas. It is a global community, welcoming people from every discipline and culture who seek a deeper understanding of the world. The power of ideas can change attitudes, lives and ultimately the world. Starting in 1984, TED Talks continue to be watched around the world, with an average of 17 new page views a second. TED conferences and events continue to inspire, motivate and thrill attendees.

TEDx are independent TED-like events, which can be organized by any inspiring and enthusiastic organizers with a license from TED. TEDx was created in the spirit of TED's mission, 'ideas worth spreading'. It supports independent organizers who want to create a TED-like event in their own community. It is designed to help communities, organizations and individuals to spark conversation and connection through local TED-like experiences. Here a screening of TED Talks videos — or a combination of live presenters and TED Talks videos — sparks deep conversation and connections at the local level. TEDx has reached a checkpoint of 100,000 talks recently.

TEDxAJCE is an independently organized TEDx conference which seeks to create the TED experience at Amal Jyothi College Of Engineering,Kanjirapally. We provide a platform where speakers can trigger a spark to the community through their words. TEDxAJCE will have experienced speakers who can share their story of success and failures which can inspire many people, for their successes. We have an amazing audience which includes enthusiastic students, professionals and the Kanjirapally community. Our aim is to reach out to the maximum and share some ideas which are worth spreading.

Jigsaw | Everything connects.

Jigsaw -
Everything Connects.

It's always the small pieces that make the big picture. Our perspectives, and their differences, make for an interesting array of solutions. It might not be realized at first with a number of seemingly unrelated pieces, but they are all interconnected. Sometimes, the big picture means looking small, finding all the pieces, and understanding that there is more to come. We ourselves can be the individual piece that completes someone else's picture. No puzzle is unsolvable, each problem has its own unique solution. The differences will determine the similarities, the similarities the connections. Everything connects.

Speakers

Nandini Rao

Improvisational & Interactive Theatre Group

Being one of the very few female theatre directors in India, Nandini Rao is undoubtedly one of the most talented women in the field. This helped her co found and be the artistic head of one of the leading theatre groups in India, Yours Truly. Yours Truly is an improvisational and interactive theatre group that has changed the face of contemporary Indian theatre with their innovative theatre styles and theatre for change as a medium.

Arjun Satheesh

Entrepreneur | Tech Enthusiast

Arjun marked the beginning of his entrepreneurial journey in college. With a passion for programming and technology right from school, Arjun was a part of multiple technology ventures both during and after his college. He dropped out of his engineering In Kerala to pursue his start-up full time. Arjun is a founding team member and the Head of Operations of CareStack, of one of the fastest growing product start-ups in US healthcare.

Nuthan Manohar

Strategist | Healer | Behavioral & Wellness Expert

Nuthan Manohar is a strategist, researcher, behavioural and wellness expert and one among the founding members of AnboduKochi. Dropping her illustrious and lucrative career she opted for the momentous breakthrough of her life when she realized the impetus of her life.She launched her firm "Me Met Me" with an impulse to refine the quality of life. Being an optimistic analyser of life, she focuses on the fact that our happiness lies within us and we need to trace our purpose.

Alphons Kannanthanam

Union Minister | Advocate

Alphons Kannanthanam is an advocate, politician and currently serving as the Union Minister of State for Electronics and Information Technology, Culture, and Tourism. "The Demolition Man". He was in TIME magazine’s list of 100 Young Global Leaders. Later he was elected as a Member of the Legislative Assembly in Kottayam District where he aspired for a progressive change. His efforts were paid when he won the best MLA award.

Sachin Warrier

Singer | Composer | Engineer

He came. He saw. And he captured the heart of Malayalees, through the song ‘Muthuchippi Poloru’, and proved his mettle as a composer through 'Anandam', giving out a bunch of beautiful 'feel good songs' which turned to be a major breakthrough in his musical career. He is a techie turned singer-cum-musician and has been a significant addition to the South Indian music industry since 2012.He is one among the few musicians known for their 'own kind of music'.

Joe A Scaria

Journalist | Trainer | Writer

A genuine and dedicated intellect Joe Scaria spends his time teaching journalism students, gives talks on media & socio economic trends and conducts workshops for corporate leaders.He was the senior assistant editor of Economic Times. With an inspiring career in media and management, spanning over 28 years, it is no wonder that Joe Scaria is an excellent orator, with the ability to send the audience into hysterics.
